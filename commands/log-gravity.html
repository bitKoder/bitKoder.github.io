Information

Falling Logs is a command contraption by koder.
The way it works is simple; You chop down a log block and the logs above it will fall down.
Falling Logs allow for much easier and more interesting tree-chopping. I hope you enjoy it!

Installation

Copy the command, then paste it into a command block in your world. Activate the command block, and the commands will spawn.
To uninstall, simply click the [Uninstall] sign.
