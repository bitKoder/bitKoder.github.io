Sign Command Toggle

Command:

Not working? Contact me on Youtube or Twitter and I'll fix it for you!

Information

This is a simple command tool by koder.
With this tool, you can create a sign-based switch for a line of commands. It has a few advantages over the switch, however.
One advantage is that the signs do not affect tick order at all, so they can be turned off and on without messing up the order of your commands.
Another advantage is that only ops can toggle the commands Finally, the player can also use the sign to describe their line of commands.
What are your thoughts on this tool? Let me know!

Installation

Copy the command, then paste it into a command block 1 block in the negative z from a sign.
Activate the command, then the sign will become a "switch" affecting the line of commands behind.
