Earth Bending

Pull blocks of earth into the air and throw them around at mobs and other players to deal damage, or use your powers more constructively to awkwardly build a set of stairs

How To Use It

Hold shift while falling to push the block in front of you up into the air when you land (if it's an earthen block), then punch the block before it falls back down to send it flying in the direction you're facing.

The blocks will deal damage based on what type of block it is (diamond ore deals the most damage, dirt the least). It can also be used for lanscaping if you're particularly brave.

Find out more about this data pack by watching the video below. Click the 'Download' button at the bottom of the screen and follow the instructions to download and install this pack, or click the 'Help' button if the pack doesn't work for you and you can't figure out why.
